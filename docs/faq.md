# FAQ

**What is the difference between HetaDB and HetaMem?**

HetaDB stores and retrieves knowledge from **human-uploaded documents** (PDFs, spreadsheets, etc.). HetaMem stores **agent-generated observations** — facts extracted from conversations (MemoryVG) and accumulated knowledge over time (MemoryKB). Think of HetaDB as your document library and HetaMem as the agent's personal memory.
